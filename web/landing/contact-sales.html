Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Typical deployment is 72 hours for sandbox environment, 2-4 weeks for full production rollout depending on your tech stack and data migration requirements.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing based on MAU (Monthly Active Users), message volume, and selected AI agents. Enterprise plans start at $5K/month. Contact sales for custom pricing.

Do you offer SOC 2 / GDPR compliance?

Yes. KIKI is SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA compliant. Our SyncShield agent provides automated DSR handling and consent management.

Can I integrate KIKI with my existing tools?

Absolutely. We have native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Shopify, SendGrid, and 50+ other platforms. RESTful APIs and webhooks for custom integrations.

What kind of support do you provide?

Enterprise customers get dedicated CSM, 24/7 technical support, Slack channel, quarterly business reviews, and priority feature requests. 99.99% uptime SLA included.

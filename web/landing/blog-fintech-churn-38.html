How We Helped a FinTech Reduce Churn by 38% in 90 Days

December 18, 2025 • 9 min read • By David Park, Engagement Lead

A technical breakdown of our PayFlow Financial deployment: architecture, data strategy, and the retention playbooks that delivered a 38% churn reduction.

← Back to Blog

Baseline

-38% churn

Net churn reduced over 90 days vs. control.

+21% NRR

Net revenue retention uplift via upsell offers to high-LTV cohorts.

+11 days

Lead time gained on churn signals vs. legacy rules.

Architecture

  • Data sources: product analytics, billing events, KYC status, support tickets.
  • LTV + churn scores served via gRPC to journey orchestration (SyncEngage).
  • Feature store freshness: 15 minutes for behavioral, 4 hours for financial.
  • Audiences synced to Braze and Twilio for multi-channel messaging.

Playbooks Launched

  • Card-decline save: proactive outreach within 30 minutes of failed payment, with smart retries and dynamic incentives.
  • Activation boost: concierge onboarding for users with low feature coverage after day 7.
  • High-value recovery: personalized offers for top LTV decile showing risk signals.
  • Support-triggered journeys: escalate unhappy tickets to success managers with retention offers.

Why It Worked

  • Better lead time: 11 extra days enabled meaningful save actions.
  • Precision: campaigns targeted only high-risk, high-value users.
  • Channel mix: email + SMS + in-app with channel fallback logic.
  • Measurement: holdout cohorts and per-playbook lift tracking.

Results

Churn dropped by 38% in 90 days; NRR improved by 21%; incentive spend decreased 19% due to precision targeting. Exec sponsor approved global rollout and added three new geos.
